French Silk Pie

Description & Origin
French Silk Pie is a rich and velvety chocolate pie with a smooth, mousse-like filling topped with whipped cream or chocolate shavings. It's a decadent dessert that's sure to impress! The origins of French Silk Pie are uncertain, but it became popular in the United States in the mid-20th century. Its silky texture and rich chocolate flavor make it a favorite at diners and bakeries.

Measured Ingredients
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 ounces unsweetened chocolate, melted and cooled
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 pre-baked pie crust, cooled
  • 1 cup heavy cream, whipped, for topping
  • Chocolate shavings, for garnish (optional)
Instructions
  1. In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Gradually add melted chocolate and vanilla extract, beating well after each addition.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, beating on medium speed for 5 minutes after each addition. (It's important to beat the mixture for the full 5 minutes after adding each egg to achieve the proper texture.)
  4. Pour filling into pre-baked pie crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
  5. Chill pie in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or until set.
  6. Before serving, top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings, if desired.
